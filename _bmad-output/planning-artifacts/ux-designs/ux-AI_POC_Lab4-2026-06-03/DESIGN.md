# DESIGN.md - AI_POC_Lab4 Search App

## Brand & Style
The visual identity is inspired by **Apple.com** with a modern **Glassmorphism** twist. It prioritizes clarity, premium typography, and a sense of depth through translucency and soft blurs.

## Colors
- **Primary Background**: A soft gradient (e.g., #f5f5f7 to #ffffff) with subtle accent hints.
- **Glass Card**: Translucent white (rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.7)) with a `backdrop-filter: blur(20px)`.
- **Primary Text**: Off-black (#1d1d1f).
- **Accent**: Apple Blue (#0071e3) for primary actions.
- **Borders**: Ultra-thin, semi-transparent white (rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.3)) for the glass effect.

## Typography
- **Font Stack**: `system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Segoe UI", Roboto, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif`.
- **Headings**: Semi-bold, tight letter-spacing.
- **Body**: Regular weight, generous line-height (1.5).

## Layout & Spacing
- **Grid**: Centered single-column layout (max-width: 800px).
- **Padding**: Large internal padding for cards and containers (24px - 32px).
- **Margins**: Significant vertical rhythm between sections.

## Shapes
- **Border Radius**: 12px for cards and containers, 8px for buttons and inputs.
- **Shadows**: Very subtle, soft shadows to create depth without clutter.

## Components
- **Inputs**: Large, clean fields with subtle borders that glow on focus.
- **Buttons**: Primary buttons are solid Blue with white text; Secondary buttons are ghost-style or light gray.
- **Table**: Minimalist table with light horizontal dividers and no vertical lines.

## Do's and Don'ts
- **Do**: Use whitespace to separate concerns.
- **Do**: Use high-quality system fonts.
- **Don't**: Use heavy borders or vibrant, clashing colors.
- **Don't**: Overcrowd the screen with unnecessary elements.
